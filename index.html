Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor in School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University. I received my PhD degree in computer science from the University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Yizhou Yu. I also hold a MS degree and a BE degree in computer science and technology from Sun Yat-sen University in 2009 and 2012 respectively. I have more than 80 publications in international conferences and journals, including TPAMI, TNNLS, TIP, CVPR, ICCV, ICML, AAAI and IJCAI, etc. My current research interests include computer vision, machine learning and medical image analysis.

I am co-supervising the HCP-I2 Lab: Human-Cyber-Physical Intelligence Integration, SYSU.

I am recruiting self-motivated Ph.D / Master students in computer vision and machine learning. If you are interested in, please directly send me your resume.

Email: liguanbin(at)mail.sysu.edu.cn
